Data Standardization Steps

Step 1: Original Data

\[ \text{data} = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 2 & 3 & 4 & 5 \end{bmatrix} \]

Step 2: Reshaped as Column Vector

\[ X = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 2 \\ 3 \\ 4 \\ 5 \end{bmatrix} \]

Step 3: Compute Mean and Standard Deviation

\[ \mu = \frac{1 + 2 + 3 + 4 + 5}{5} = 3 \]
\[ \sigma = \sqrt{\frac{(1-3)^2 + (2-3)^2 + (3-3)^2 + (4-3)^2 + (5-3)^2}{5}} = \sqrt{2} \approx 1.414 \]

Step 4: Standardized Values

\[ Z = \frac{X - \mu}{\sigma} \]
\[ Z = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{1 - 3}{1.414} \\ \frac{2 - 3}{1.414} \\ \frac{3 - 3}{1.414} \\ \frac{4 - 3}{1.414} \\ \frac{5 - 3}{1.414} \end{bmatrix} \]
\[ Z \approx \begin{bmatrix} -1.414 \\ -0.707 \\ 0.000 \\ 0.707 \\ 1.414 \end{bmatrix} \]
